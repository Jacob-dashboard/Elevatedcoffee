# Elevated Coffee — Social Media Content Plan

## Brand Voice
- **Tone:** Confident, minimal, aspirational — not hype-y
- **Personality:** The friend who's ahead of the curve. Sharp. Calm. Knows something you don't yet.
- **Avoid:** Overcaffeinated energy drink vibes, bro culture, overly scientific jargon
- **Lean into:** Aesthetic, focus, intentionality, the "quiet achiever" archetype

## Platforms

| Platform | Priority | Content Type | Post Frequency |
|---|---|---|---|
| Instagram | Primary | Feed posts, Reels, Stories | 5x/week |
| TikTok | Primary | Short-form video | 4x/week |
| X (Twitter) | Secondary | Text + image, brand voice | 3x/week |
| Pinterest | Supporting | Aesthetic boards, lifestyle | 2x/week |

## Content Pillars (rotate through these)

1. **Product** — The cans. Beauty shots. Flavor features. Ingredients.
2. **Lifestyle** — Who drinks Elevated and when. Morning routines, work sessions, creative flow.
3. **Education** — Adaptogens explained. What L-Theanine actually does. Why no crash.
4. **Culture** — Aesthetic. Quotes. Mood. Brand world-building.
5. **Social Proof** — UGC reposts, reviews, testimonials.

## Content Creation Notes

### Aesthetic Direction
- **Colors:** Dark backgrounds (black, deep navy), cream/off-white text, warm amber accents
- **Typography:** Mix of serif (Playfair-style) for headlines, clean sans-serif for body
- **Lighting:** Hard directional light, condensation on cans, cinematic
- **Mood:** Premium, calm, intentional — NOT energy drink chaos

### Shot List (for photo/video day)
1. All 3 cans on black surface — overhead flat lay
2. Single can hero shots (each flavor)
3. Can-in-hand lifestyle (desk, outdoors, morning light)
4. Close-up: condensation on can
5. Can cracked open — pour shot
6. Ingredient flat lay: coffee beans, mushroom, ashwagandha root
7. Morning routine sequence
8. "Work session" desk setup with can

### Reels/TikTok Format
- Hook in first 2 seconds (text overlay or spoken)
- 15-30 second sweet spot
- Trending audio when possible
- Always end with brand name + tagline
